Ankit Garg

 

I am a researcher at Microsoft Research India, Bengaluru since July 2018. Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Microsoft Research New England in Cambridge, MA. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from Princeton University where I was fortunate to be advised by Mark Braverman. I obtained my B-Tech in Computer Science from IIT Delhi.

I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science, specifically in algebraic complexity, communication complexity and continuous optimization.
